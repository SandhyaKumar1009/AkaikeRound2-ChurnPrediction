# AkaikeRound2-ChurnPrediction
Business Insights
Key factors driving customer churn:
 *Age affects churn
 *IsActiveMember strongly impacts churn
 *Customers with fewer products churn more
 *Balance per product has impact

Customer segments at highest risk:
 Inactive members
 Customers with only 1 product
 Customers residing in Germany

Recommendations to reduce churn:
 Conducting campaigns to increase customers
 Offer more premium offers for the more loyal customers
